# Interoduction to Clients & Transports [What is Clients & Transports.]


## Client

A `Client` provides access to a subset of `Actions`.

> You can find more about `Clients` in the [Viem Clients](https://viem.sh/docs/clients/intro#clients).

## Transport

A Client is instantiated with a Transport, which is the intermediary layer that is responsible for executing outgoing requests (ie. RPC requests).

> You can find more about `Transports` in the [Viem Transports](https://viem.sh/docs/clients/intro#transports).
